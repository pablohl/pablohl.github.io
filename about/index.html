About

Hi, I’m Pablo, I’m currently a machine learning researcher at Borealis AI which is part of RBC (Royal Bank of Canada) in Toronto, Canada. I’ve worked on fundamental research in multiagent deep reinforcement learning (for example in this NeurIPS Pommerman competition), auxiliary tasks for Deep RL and robustness in RL. In terms of applied research I have worked on explainability for an RL agent that is trading in markets.

Previously, I was part of the Intelligent and Autonomous Systems of CWI in Amsterdam, the Netherlands working with Michael Kaisers.
